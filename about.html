We’ve all heard the saying: “Hindsight is 20/20.”

Starting out your career in the creative field can be fun and exciting, but with that, there are loads of new experiences, questions, and inevitable mistakes to be made. Whenever I get emails in my inbox from fresh creatives asking for advice, I often respond with the things I would tell my younger self as I was just starting out. I’ve been wanting to find a way to spread this at a broader level to other new creatives, so I spun up Hindsight.

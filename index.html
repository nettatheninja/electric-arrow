Q: What advice would you give your younger self as you were just starting out?

You're a beginner; keep that mindset even when it's mastered.

Jump off the cliff, your best lessons are at the bottom.

Your design is bad, you're not a bad designer (or person).

Find a mentor and don't be afraid to ask for help.

Learn the fundamentals of your craft first. You can't paint a house without a frame.

— Justin Severson, Writer
